Critical Maintenance / Breakdown Log

Report, triage and close out equipment breakdowns — the reactive half of the maintenance program

Critical maintenance breakdown log scoped by equipment category, type and asset, with severity triage (Concerning/Needs technician/Repair/Urgent/Immediate), status tracking, downtime state, assigned technician, notes, photos and parts used (parts chosen from a dropdown, with an add-to-inventory workflow), plus a report-breakdown action and a double-click or edit-icon popup for updating a breakdown record.

Selecting a category, type or asset scopes the breakdown log below to that equipment. Equipment itself can only be added or edited on the Equipment / Part Detail page.
Double-click a row — or use the edit icon — to update status, add notes, log parts used, or close it out.
